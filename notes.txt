    The plugin performs real time analysis of all comments on a Yt video , classifyimg each as positive , neutral or negative.

-> sentiment Distribution Visualisation
    Displays the overall sentiment Distribution with intuitive graphs or charts

    Allows users to drill down into each sentiment category to read specific comments classified under it.

-> Trend tracking
    Monitors how sentiment changes over time  , helping influencers identify how different conetnt affects audience perception.
